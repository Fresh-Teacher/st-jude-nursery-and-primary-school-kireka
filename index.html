F.A.Q

  • Does St. Jude enroll Catholic pupils only?

    Not really! Despite the fact that our school is a Catholic founded school, we welcome all learners from different doctrines.

  • Does your school give time to learners to rest and refresh their minds?

    Yes! We organise different occassions to give entertainment to our learners like conducting tours and trips to different parts of the country where pupils go for study purpose and fun!

  • Can your school provide accomodation facilities to my child who lives far away from the school?

    Yes! Our school has dormitories for boarding pupils so you can bring you child in boarding section.

  • Do you also teach infants?

    Yes! Our school has a nursery section with well trained professional teachers who handle young children.

  • Since your school is a Catholic founded school, won't my child from a different religion be discriminated?

    Not at all! Our schools respects all religions and gives time for pupils from different religions to worship their God on the specified days of worship.

  • Can I bring my child for registration now?

    Registration is already in progress! Please register your child with us today.
